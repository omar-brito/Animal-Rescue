Our organization was founded on the belief that animals are entitled to kind and respectful treatment at the hand of humans. The AHSC mission is to provide shelter, medical attention, and the effective means for the prevention of cruelty to animals throughout the United States.

Keep Them Safe

Rex

We thought the process would take a while to find a pet that fit with our family, but we met Rex that very first night. Our son, Paul, knew right away that Rex was right for our family, and insisted that he take him home.

The last two months have been pure joy for our family. Paul refers to Rex as his “twin brother” Rex rarely leaves Paul’s side, and has brought so much fun and laughter to our house!

Apr 10, 2020

Brandy

Brandy came into our home knowing he had certain health condition declining. Considering he was 14 years old, this wasn’t a surprise. My husband and I decided to adopt him with the hope of making his final days as comfortable as possible.

We fell in love with him and watched his health improve gradually. He loves sitting in the sunshine, walking around the back yard, eating, snuggling with our 2-year-old son, and spending time with his brother, Jango He is a member of our family and we are so happy we decided to take him!

Jan 15, 2021

Georgia

To this day, I swear that she picked me. She has brought joy and light into my life, has reminded me what unconditional love looks like, and is the best little side kick a girl could ask for! She comes with me to work every day. She has helped bring love and joy into the lives of others who, like me, struggled for so long to find their way.

I am forever grateful for AHSC, and this little love-bug of mine.

Feb 02, 2021
